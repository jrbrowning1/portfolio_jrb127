Growing up a Duke basketball fan, I developed a love for watching sports from a young age. As I got older, my tastes began to branch outside of just Duke basketball and I started to enjoy other sports, including football and tennis (go Nadal). But despite all the time I spent watching sports, I never gave a second thought to writing about them. That changed when I got to Duke. I went to a soccer game during my orientation week and was quickly hooked, realizing that there were so many other sports out there I hadn't given a chance. The next semester I joined The Chronicle's Sports Department and have since written about a number of sports.
