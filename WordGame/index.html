Let's play Jotto!

How to play

Pick a five letter word (in your head or write it down) and I will try to guess it! All you have to do is tell me how many letters of your word my guess matches. Think of it like 20 questions, but for words (and I ask the same question every time but that's beside the point). Depending on the difficulty, I have to guess it in under a certain number of tries to win - otherwise you win! But be warned - I'm pretty good at this :)
(Note: the higher you put the difficulty, the more guesses I get to try and read your mind!)
